Box Sizing

Let's compare box-sizing: content-box and box-sizing: border-box. Each square in the grid below is 20px × 20px.

Content Box

The 20px of padding and border are added on top of the 240px width and height of this box. Surprisingly, the box takes up 320px × 320px of space.

Border Box

This box is actually 240px × 240px. The 20px of border and padding are included inside the height, rather than added on.
